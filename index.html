League of Heroes The League of Heroes is a collection of some of Earth’s mightiest costumed super-heroes! We’ve decided to use of powers for the forces of good to fight evil in all its forms. We believe in changing the world, and we believe the best way to do that is in tights and a cape! The League is the words most powerful collection of crime fighters.
The League of Heroes began operations in October 2013. Located at 1234 Do-gooders Way in Toronto's tech district, we have established ourselves as Canada's first and foremost super team.

Its founder, Dr. Goliath, has since been instrumental in collecting heroes from around the country including Shriek, Ironjaw, Power-Maiden, Tom from Accounting, and the mysterious Shroud.The League’s sixth member joined last year. Boasting 3 laser projecting eye cannons, "Tricolops" was an immediate crowd favourite. His distinctive costume and loud war cry set him apart from other heroes, and gained him an instant following.
Since that time, the League has steadily expanded its roster. This winter, we intend on introducing our latest hero (keep watching the skies for C.L.A.M.P in December).

The League was founded by Dr. Goliath's belief that he could make a better world, and that the law abiding public deserve more. Although he had been a part time hero for more than 25 years, it was only during the early 2000s that he began seriously considering going full time. In August 2013, he left his position as a senior webmaster at Seneca College and dedicated himself to turning his vision into reality.
